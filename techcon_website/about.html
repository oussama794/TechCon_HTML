Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts. Since then, it has grown into a premier international tech conference attracting innovators from across the globe.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire, connect, and educate professionals across all areas of technology. We focus on innovation, inclusion, and the future of tech.
